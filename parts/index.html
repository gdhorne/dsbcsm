Data Scientist's Toolbox

The foundational course, Data Scientist's Toolbox, is a high-level overview of the specialisation. This course lays the groundwork for the nine-course series plus capstone project. We will learn basic skills founded on data science methodology rather than data science technique using a toolset. Throughout boot-camp and the data specialisation courses this toolbox will be our survival kit.

Welcome to Boot-Camp

Welcome recruits!

I have served as a Community Teaching Assistant (CTA) and Community Mentor for various courses in the Data Science Specialization offered by Johns Hopkins University between May 2014 and April 2016.

The Data Science Specialization is a challenging nine-course sequence focusing on the fundamentals of data science. Success requires a strategy. The purpose of this blog to support each of you with the knowledge and practical skills to complete the specialisation. The Data Science Boot-Camp Survival Manual has been developed independently of Johns Hopkins University and Coursera. It is provided as a convenience to participants in the Data Science Specialization and as a practical introduction to data science for the general public.

Read the full introduction to the blog in the article Data Science Boot-Camp to discover how it aligns with the Data Science Specialization.
